Mergre Round Car

Merge Round Racers is a car fusion game created by TinyDobbins where players have to make money, merge 2 similar cars to upgrade to a better one, and let those cars race against each other. Each time completing a race, players will be rewarded with a certain amount of coins depending on the level at that time. Try to create as many cars as possible to make more coins.
